About the pie

Selector Pie is a DJ, producer, and beatmaker focused on one thing: making sounds people want to play on repeat. Every beat starts as a small idea — a loop, a drum pattern, a sample — and gets built into something full.

Right now the focus is on production: building out a catalogue of original instrumentals, sharpening the craft, and putting beats out into the world for anyone who wants to vibe to them — from Europe to the Caribbean and beyond.
