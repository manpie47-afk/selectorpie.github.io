The Sound

From Carnival Anthems To Cinematic Electronics

Selector Pie is a music producer, composer, and sound designer from Trinidad & Tobago, known for crafting energetic Caribbean-inspired productions that blend Soca, Dancehall, Bouyon, EDM, and Shatta influences.

Specializing in original riddims, instrumentals, remixes, and custom productions, Selector Pie delivers powerful rhythms, memorable melodies, and professional-quality sound for artists, DJs, content creators, and music libraries worldwide.

Driven by innovation and a deep love for music production, the catalog keeps growing, connecting Caribbean culture with international audiences, one riddim at a time.

"Creating music that moves people, from the Caribbean to the world." 🌎🎵
— Selector Pie
